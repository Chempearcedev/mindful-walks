About Us



We offers free guided walks in and around East Sussex. My name is Mo and i am the guide that will be taking you on our walks. I am a trained guide with experience guided people around the mountains in splan and decided to bring my skills to East Sussex. I am an advocate for mental health, i work with people with special needs and i am training to be a counsellor. I want to bring my skills to support individuals in East Sussex, an area i grew u in. Our ambition is to increase and support physical and mental wellbeing through walking.

Our walking groups are targeted towards specific groups of people such as the over 50's, families, individuals with physical and mental learning difficulties and mental health. However, everyone is welcome on our walks. All our guided walks are free and we are mindful about individuals needs and circumstances and ensure that we consider this when choosing the location, length and terrain of our walks. East Sussex offers some of the most beautiful locations to walk in across the UK. Our missions for the future is to encourage physical and mental wellbeing across different demographics and areas.
