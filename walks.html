Walking Groups And Times

  • Over 50's in Seven Sister's
    Seven Sisters: CLICK ME! Saturday's 12pm Meeting at: Seven Sisters Country Park Car Park
    Our over 50's walk through the beautiful countryside of Seven Sisters offers an opportunity to meet new people, socialise and grap a cuppa along the way. Often people people can feel isolated and lonely, this walk is design to tackle this issue whilst getting our well needed physical exercise. Our main aim is for our group members to make new friends. Each walk is carefully considered in relation to length, terrain and location to allow everyone to get stuck in. Our guide will show you the beautiful countryside of Seven Sisters and knows all the best coffee stops along the way. Most of our walks are 1 mile long, however we often do half that if we want more time to chat. Please join us!
  • Mental Health group
    Devil's Dyke: CLICK ME! Sunday's 12pm Meeting at: Devil's Dyke Pub
    It has been scientifically proven that physical activity can increase our mental health. This groups is for people who suffer from mental health or supports the cause. Our guide will take you along the wonderful area of Devils Dyke and show and enjoy the views with you. Our aim is to increase invididuals mental and physical wellbeing through walking with like minded people. Our walks come in various distances, often decided on the day depending on the groups needs. Please join us!
  • Special Needs Group
    Ditchling Beacon: CLICK ME Wednesday 12pm Meeting at: Ditchling Beacon Village Hall Car Park
    Our special needs group meets in Ditchling Beacon and explores the beautiful coast line whilst getting some well needed physical activity. All needs groups are welcome, we have carefully considered the length, duration and terrain of the walks to allow access to wheelchairs and those with physically restricted conditions. We ensure that we have a few more volunteers on the day to support anything you may need. Please join in!
  • Family Group
    Burling Gap: CLICK ME! Wednesday's 12pm Meeting at: Tiger Inn
    Our family walk is designed to allow access to free guided walks for families to enable better physical health. Often parents can find it difficult to find something to do with the kids that. Our guided walks will showcase the besutiful area of Burling Gap whilst providing you with historical information about the area and encourage curiosity and adventure. Our aim to increase families physical health whilst providing an opportunity to meet new people and make new friends. Please join us!
